Table of Contents#

This book is split into multiple parts. Each part covers a complete topic in the QIIME 2 ecosystem. You do not need to master all of these sections to develop with QIIME 2. For example, if you are interested in creating plugins, then the only section you need to concern yourself with is the Plugins section. Other topics, such as Framework Internals and CI Internals, are advanced topics that are mostly useful for the QIIME 2 Development Team itself.
